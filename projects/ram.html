The Battering Ram

I made this model for the Arms Race 2 mod contest for Team Fortress 2. It is meant to work as a shield the character uses to charge through enemies.
It features hand-painted textures and low-poly modeling.

Programs used: Zbrush, 3dsmax, Photoshop, Source Engine

Tasks: Designing, modeling and texturing the weapon




The last picture was rendered in Source Film Maker then edited in Photoshop, and shows how the model looks in context with Valve's characters.
