Stratosphere

School project at NAD where we had to make a playable chase scene in 7 weeks. I was in charge of the characters.
We aimed for an art style that was realistic, but more saturated and less noisy than photorealism. The theme we went for was a sci-fi twist on Aztec ruins.

Programs used: 3dsmax, Zbrush, xNormal, Substance Painter, Unreal Engine

Tasks: Modeling and texturing the 2 characters, designing the snake god
