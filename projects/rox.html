Rox Willful

The protagonist for my final project at NAD, a comedic point & click game set in space. She's a charismatic reporter who crashed on an asteroid. The model has a full facial rig.
We plan on publishing the game on Steam.

Programs used: 3dsmax, Zbrush, Substance Painter, Unreal Engine

Tasks: Modeling and texturing the character, game design





Other credits:
-Character design and 2D backgrounds by Gabriel Kelly
-Animation by Jean-Christophe Vanasse and Maxime Deroy
