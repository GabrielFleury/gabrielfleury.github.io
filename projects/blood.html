Blood

School project at NAD where we had to remake/reboot an old PC game in 7 weeks. Our team picked Blood. We went for an art style that focuses on chaos, contrasts and silhouettes.
I was in charge of the 2 characters and their skinning. Both characters use cloth physics.

Programs used: Zbrush, 3dsmax, xNormal, Substance Painter, Photoshop, Unreal 4 Engine

Tasks: Modeling and texturing the 2 characters
