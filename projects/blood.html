Blood

School project at NAD where we had to remake/reboot an old PC game in 7 weeks.
Our team picked Blood. We went for an art style that focuses on chaos, contrasts and silhouettes.
See the end result of the project in this video.

Programs used: Zbrush, 3dsmax, xNormal, Substance Painter, Photoshop, Unreal 4 Engine

Tasks: Modeling, texturing and skinning the 2 characters. Both characters use cloth physics.
